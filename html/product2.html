FAQ ▼

How is this whey protein isolate protein different to other whey proteins?
Whey protein can come in various forms, most commonly whey protein isolate (WPI) and whey protein concentrate (WPC). Whey protein isolate is a very high quality form of whey protein which undergoes additional processing relative to whey protein concentrate. This results in a higher protein percentage and a lower fat and carbohydrate percentage. WPI is a superior protein to be consuming post-workout to help facilitate recovery and muscle growth for this reason. It is also more expensive than its WPC counterpart. Many whey proteins contain either WPC, or a blend between WPI and WPC. These other whey proteins are not of an equivalent quality. Simply look at the nutritional panel to see what type of whey protein(s) are used in the product.
