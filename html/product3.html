What is Casein protein?
Casein protein is a protein found in cow's milk. When carrying the same amino acid profile as whey protein, casein protein is absorbed at a different rate. Casein protein is a slow-releasing protein. In other words, after ingestion, your muscles will be 'drip-fed' casein for several hours. Casein protein is often considered 'anticatabolic' as it provides a steadier stream of acids and protein supply to prevent muscle breakdown.
